안녕Manchester United Football Club

Manchester United Football Club is a professional football club based in the Old Trafford area of Manchester, England.

The club competes in the Premier League, the top flight of English football.

Nicknamed the Red Devils, it was founded as Newton Heath LYR Football Club in 1878, but changed its name to Manchester United in 1902. The club moved from its ground in Newton Heath to its current stadium, Old Trafford, in 1910.
